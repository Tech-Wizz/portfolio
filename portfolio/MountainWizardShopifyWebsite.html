Mountain Wizard Shopify Website

Overview

This version of the Mountain Wizard website was built using Shopify to achieve a more commercial, scalable online storefront. Unlike the earlier custom HTML GitHub Pages site, the Shopify version focuses on professional retail design, integrated fulfillment flows, and automated business systems. This setup allows Mountain Wizard LLC to operate like a modern e‑commerce brand with minimal manual overhead.

The store incorporates print‑on‑demand vendors, social media shops, automated marketing workflows, and a dedicated blog for improved SEO and storytelling.

Key Objectives

  • Develop a commercial‑grade Shopify storefront with a modern theme
  • Integrate Printful, PrintNinja, and other PoD providers for fulfillment
  • Enable clean and consistent social media links across all pages
  • Create social media store integrations for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, etc.
  • Automate emails, SMS, and product collections using Shopify tools and apps
  • Add a company blog for product updates, behind‑the‑scenes, and SEO

Platform & Technologies

  • Shopify – Hosted eCommerce platform with theme customization
  • Liquid template language – Dynamic product page and theme editing
  • Printful / PrintNinja integrations – PoD product creation + fulfillment
  • Shopify Flow + Email – Business automation engine
  • SMS platforms – Automated text notifications
  • Meta / TikTok / Pinterest Shops – Social commerce connectivity
  • Shopify Blog – Content system for organic reach

Print‑On‑Demand Integrations

Mountain Wizard offers products through a network of print‑on‑demand distributors. Shopify makes it possible to fully automate print jobs, fulfillment, tracking, and shipping notifications.

  • Printful – Apparel, accessories, lifestyle products
  • PrintNinja – Custom print runs and specialty items
  • Other PoD Providers – Integrated through Shopify app ecosystem

Products route automatically based on vendor rules, which reduces errors and ensures timely production.

Social Media + Social Commerce Integrations

The Shopify website integrates deeply with social media platforms, allowing Mountain Wizard to sell products directly inside apps customers already use. The interfaces provide cleaner profile links, icons, and consistent branding.

  • Instagram Shop – Tap‑to‑view product tagging
  • Facebook Shop – Auto‑synced product catalog
  • TikTok Shop – In‑video shoppable links
  • Pinterest Rich Pins – Automatic product metadata

Automation Systems

A major advantage of Shopify over the previous version is its built‑in automation ecosystem. I configured several automated systems to reduce manual management.

  • Email Automation – Welcome series, abandoned cart, win‑back flows
  • Automated Collections – Smart groupings using rules based on tags, vendor, or price
  • SMS Messaging – Marketing texts and order updates
  • Shopify Flow – Vendor‑based fulfillment routing, tagging, and alerts

Blog Integration

The Shopify site includes a dedicated blog used for product updates, behind‑the‑scenes content, and evergreen informational posts about Mountain Wizard products. This increases organic visibility and strengthens brand identity.

  • Categories: Product Releases, Process Notes, Announcements
  • SEO‑friendly formatting with structured headers and metadata
  • Embedded images and videos to enhance reader engagement

Project Highlights

  • Commercial Shopify redesign with modern UX and cleaner navigation
  • Multi‑provider print‑on‑demand fulfillment pipeline
  • Native social media storefront integrations
  • Automated workflows for email, SMS, and merchandising
  • Added blog for continued content growth and organic reach
